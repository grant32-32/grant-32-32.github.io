What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that enables machines to mimic human intelligence. AI is everywhere online, from chatbots to recommendation engines, virtual assistants, and even self-driving cars.

How AI Works on the Internet

AI helps websites understand user behavior, provide smart search results, detect spam, and improve online experiences. Chatbots like ChatGPT use AI to answer questions, help with coding, or even create art.

Fun Facts About AI & the Internet

AI powers personalized recommendations on platforms like YouTube, Netflix, and TikTok.

AI algorithms learn from huge amounts of data collected globally to improve accuracy.

Virtual assistants like Siri or Alexa are powered by AI and natural language processing.

AI can detect spam emails and fake news faster than humans.

Machine learning helps social media suggest friends, posts, and content you might like.

AI art generators can create paintings, music, and even memes in seconds!

Some AI chatbots can tutor students or help with language learning online.
